**VALERIO:**

**[Count on fingers as listing]**

> "It reads the patient's clinical context. It pre-fills prescription renewals based on treatment history. It suggests referrals following clinical guidelines. It drafts discharge summaries using standardized medical language."

**[Look back at camera - emphasize]**

**[VISUAL: Medical authority - this is the credibility moment]**

**VALERIO:**

**[VISUAL CUT TO: Screen showing Diraya interface with patient queue - anonymized - 5 seconds]**

**[VISUAL: Back to Valerio holding a medical chart]**

**VALERIO:**

**VALERIO:**

**[VISUAL: Data-backed, confident tone]**

**VALERIO:**
